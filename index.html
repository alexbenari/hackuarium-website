The Hackuarium is a unique exhibition space located at the Weizmann Institute of Science.
It displays a single work of digital art at any given time, each for a period several weeks.
The works are all site-specific in the sense that they are attuned to the Hackuarium's unique means of display: two large screens suspended behind a high black glass wall.

This website will host digital versions of past works, information about the artists and content about related topics such as digital diptychs, the digital as a form of material and more.
